## Strategic Filter Questions

Before committing to any task, decision, or initiative, ask:

1. **Does this directly address the current Bottleneck?**
2. **Does this move toward the One Objective?**
3. **Does this contribute to the One Goal?**

If the answer to all three is no — kill it, park it in Someday/Maybe, or delegate it.

---

## OOBG Scoring (for Prioritization Engine)

When scoring tasks against the OOBG:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🔴 Critical | Directly addresses the Bottleneck OR directly enables the Objective |
| 🟡 Important | Necessary but not bottleneck-breaking. Do after Critical tasks. |
| 🟢 Maintenance | Required to keep existing commitments. Schedule, don't prioritize. |
| ⚪ Question | Unclear connection. Challenge it before doing it. |

---

## Review Cadence

- **Weekly** — Does the Bottleneck still accurately describe the #1 obstacle? Update during `/review` Weekly Synthesis.
- **Monthly** — Full OOBG review during Monthly Strategic Reset. Has the Objective shifted? Has the Goal moved?
- **Quarterly** — Deep review. Is the OOBG still the right one? Does anything in the direction need to change?
