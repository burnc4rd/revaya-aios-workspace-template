# outputs/

> Work products and deliverables.
>
> Organize by project, client, or type. This is where research briefs, content drafts, proposals, and deliverables live.
>
> **Common subdirectories:**
> - `outputs/research/` — research briefs from `/research`, `/reddit-research`, `/competitive-research`
> - `outputs/content/` — drafted posts and content from `/draft-post`, `/draft-content`
> - `outputs/ops/` — internal dashboards, process docs, runbooks
>
> Outputs are not committed by default unless they're important deliverables. See `.gitignore`.
