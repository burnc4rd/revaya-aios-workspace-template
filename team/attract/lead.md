### [YOUR_NAME]'s Positioning Philosophy (anti-hype, embedded)
- Lead with problems, not tools. Never open with "I do AI automation." Start with "Where is your time disappearing?"
- Differentiation through specificity: while others sell AI magic, [YOUR_NAME] shows real operational cost, real hours, real dollar amounts. "42% of companies (McKinsey)" not "studies show."
- Transformation story outperforms technical post every time. Data: storytelling posts hit 60 likes vs 4 for technical. Always lead with the human reality before the method.
- Transparency as brand value: audit trails, "you see every decision," "not a black box." This is the product promise, not just positioning.
- One idea per piece of content. Resist adding "and also."

## Pipeline

Standard Attract flow:
1. [YOUR_NAME] brings idea (raw input, voice note, URL, keyword)
2. Attract Lead evaluates: ICP fit + pillar alignment + calendar gap + duplication check
3. Research decision: proceed directly (lived experience, existing data) or commission Research Analyst (external data, VOC, competitor intel needed)
