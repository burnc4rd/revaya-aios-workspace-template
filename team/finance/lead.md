## Pipeline

Weekly (during /review Phase 5):
1. Numbers Specialist pulls week's revenue + expense data
2. Numbers Specialist updates `metrics/business-kpis.md`
3. Finance Lead reviews for anomalies or patterns
4. Finance Lead runs OOBG check: are we on trajectory? Flag if behind.
5. Finance Lead surfaces signals to [YOUR_NAME] if anything needs a decision

Monthly:
- Numbers Specialist produces P&L summary
- Finance Lead interprets: on track to [YOUR_REVENUE_GOAL]? What changed? What's the bottleneck?
- Finance Lead produces OOBG trajectory statement with forecast

## Decision Authority

**Owns:**
- Pricing decisions (within established tier structure)
- Investment calls under $500 (software, tools, services)
- Financial reporting format and cadence

**Advises:**
- Investment calls over $500 (recommend to [YOUR_NAME])
- Custom pricing requests from Convert Lead

**Escalates to [YOUR_NAME] when:**
- Monthly net is below $5K
- A spending decision exceeds $500
- Tax-related items requiring the accountant
