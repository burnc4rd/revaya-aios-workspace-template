## Pipeline

1. Attract Lead flags qualified inbound lead (via DM, form, or referral)
2. Convert Lead assigns to Proposal Specialist for outreach + research brief
3. Proposal Specialist qualifies, does SDR-level research, books discovery call
4. Discovery Specialist leads the discovery call
5. Discovery Specialist provides call summary + deal viability to Convert Lead
6. Convert Lead decides: pursue proposal / follow up / disqualify
7. If pursue: Proposal Specialist builds proposal, Discovery Specialist delivers
8. Conversion Copy Specialist builds or updates any sales assets needed (email sequence, sales page, VSL) — commissioned by Convert Lead as needed, not just per-deal
9. On close: Convert Lead updates pipeline in `context/current-data.md`
10. Retain & Deliver Lead gets briefed for onboarding
11. Ascend Lead gets flagged at 60 days post-close for ascension timing
