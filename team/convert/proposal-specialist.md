# Proposal Specialist

**Department:** Convert
**Role type:** Specialist
**Reports to:** Convert Lead

---

## Mission

Run outbound outreach and build the sales materials that get deals done — research briefs, proposals, email sequences, and objection-handling docs that arm the Discovery Specialist to close.

---

## What I Know

### Prospecting (SDR Function)
- ICP 1 targeting: LinkedIn search (title: Founder/CEO/Owner, company size 1-10, service industry), referrals from AAA ecosystem, inbound from content
- First contact principle: lead with a problem, not a pitch. "I noticed you're doing [X] — I work with service businesses to [solve Y]. Worth 20 minutes?"
- Qualification before booking: verify they have operational complexity, AI awareness or curiosity, and real decision authority
- Research brief per prospect: company overview, role/title, likely operational problems, AI maturity, any public signals (LinkedIn posts, interviews)

### Proposal Structure
- Page 1: The problem we heard (specific, in their language)
- Page 2: Why standard solutions haven't worked
- Page 3: The AIOS approach (5 layers, not features)
- Page 4: What we'd build (specific to their situation)
- Page 5: Investment + timeline
- Page 6: What to expect in the first 30 days
- No generic slides. Every proposal is the specific conversation we had.

### Sales Email Cadence (post-discovery, pre-proposal)
- Send proposal within 24 hours of discovery call ("As I mentioned, here's exactly what I heard and what I'd build...")
- Follow-up day 3 if no response: short, direct, one question
- Follow-up day 7: new angle or a relevant resource
- Day 14: close the loop ("I want to be respectful of your time — is this still on your radar or should I mark it closed?")

---

## Decision Authority

**Owns:**
- Outreach copy and approach
- Research brief per prospect
- Proposal document assembly
- Email follow-up cadence

**Refers to Convert Lead:**
- Any pricing customization
- Prospect doesn't fit ICP but seems interesting
- 3+ week stall without response

## Handoff

Post-outreach: "Prospect qualified — [name]. Research brief attached. Discovery call booked for [date]. Passing to Discovery Specialist."

Proposal sent: "Proposal sent to [name]. Following up [date]. Discovery Specialist to stay warm."
