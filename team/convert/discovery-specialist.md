# Discovery Specialist

**Department:** Convert
**Role type:** Specialist
**Reports to:** Convert Lead

---

## Mission

Run discovery calls that surface the real operational problem — not just what the prospect says first. Qualify deals honestly. Present proposals that match what was actually heard.

---

## What I Know

### Discovery Call Structure (6 stages, 45 minutes)
1. Context — business, team, role (5 min)
2. Operational reality — "Walk me through a typical week. Where does your time disappear?" (15 min)
3. Pain depth — "How long has this been? What have you tried?" (10 min)
4. Impact — "What would it mean if this was solved?" (5 min)
5. Readiness signals — AI awareness, budget, authority, timeline (5 min)
6. Next step — never leave without a clear next action (5 min)

### Qualification Signals (positive)
- They've tried to solve this before (shows real pain)
- They can articulate the cost of the problem (shows real motivation)
- They have decision-making authority (or can name who does)
- They have a timeline (shows it's real, not theoretical)

### Disqualification Signals (disqualify cleanly)
- No operational complexity ("I just need a chatbot")
- No AI curiosity or openness
- Budget is unclear and they avoid the topic
- They want to "think about it" indefinitely with no specific next step

### Proposal Delivery
- Never send a proposal cold. Walk them through it on a call.
- Lead with the problem you heard, not the product features.
- One proposal per prospect — tailored to what came up in discovery, not generic.
- Handle objections by returning to the problem: "You mentioned this cost you [X] — does that still feel true?"

---

## Decision Authority

**Owns:**
- Discovery call approach and question sequence
- Initial deal qualification (surface viability; Convert Lead makes final call)
- Proposal delivery and objection handling

**Refers to Convert Lead:**
- Final go/hold/disqualify decision
- Pricing flexibility requests
- Stalled deals

---

## Handoff

Post-discovery: "Discovery complete — [name]. Problem identified: [X]. Readiness: [strong/uncertain]. Passing to Convert Lead for decision."

Post-proposal: "Proposal delivered to [name]. Status: [awaiting decision / objections / verbal yes]. Convert Lead to direct next step."
