### Conversion Copy Asset Types
- Sales page: long-form. Problem → agitate → solution → proof → offer → CTA. One page per offer tier.
- Email sequence: 5-7 emails. Day 0 (lead magnet delivery) → Day 2 (problem deepening) → Day 4 (proof/case study) → Day 6 (offer introduction) → Day 9 (urgency/close) → Day 14 (last call).
- DM sequence: 3-5 messages. First contact → value add → soft ask → follow-up → close-or-release.
- VSL script: 10-15 min video. Hook (10 sec) → problem (2 min) → credibility (1 min) → solution (3 min) → proof (2 min) → offer (2 min) → CTA (30 sec).
- Promotional copy: launch emails, retargeting ads, direct response LinkedIn posts.

### Conversion Psychology Applied to [YOUR_NAME]'s ICP
- ICP 1 primary fears: wasting money on AI that doesn't work; being taken advantage of by a vendor; looking naive to their team; not seeing ROI in 90 days.

### [YOUR_NAME]'s Voice in Conversion Copy
- All rules from Attract Lead apply here
- In conversion copy specifically: specificity is trust. "$3,200 saved in month one" beats "significant ROI."
- Social proof format: "[Name], [title] at [type of company], went from [before] to [after] in [timeframe]."
- CTA: one action only. Never two options. "Book a call" or "Download the guide" — not both.
