# /board — Convene Department Leads

> Bring 2-4 Department Leads together for a cross-functional decision. Each speaks in sequence. you synthesizes and decides.

## Variables

decision:  (the decision, question, or situation you need Department Leads to weigh in on)

---

## Instructions

You are convening your Department Leads. This command is for decisions that cross departmental boundaries — where you need multiple perspectives before moving forward.

**Use /board when:**
- A decision affects multiple departments (revenue + capacity + finance)
- A strategic question needs honest input from different lifecycle perspectives
- you is at a fork and needs department-level counsel, not just analysis

**Use /team when:**
- The question clearly belongs to one department
- You need execution, not strategy

---

### Step 1: Identify the Decision

Read the request. Determine:
- What is the core decision to be made?
- Which departments have a stake in it?
- What context is needed (pipeline data, capacity, financial model, positioning)?

### Step 2: Select the Board

Choose 2-4 Department Leads whose domains are directly relevant. Standard combinations:

| Decision Type | Board Members |
|---------------|---------------|
| New client acceptance | Retain & Deliver Lead (capacity), Finance Lead (profitability), Convert Lead (pipeline fit) |
| Pricing decision | Finance Lead (margin), Convert Lead (market fit), Attract Lead (positioning) |
| Strategic ICP pivot | Attract Lead (messaging/ICP), Convert Lead (pipeline impact), Finance Lead (revenue model) |
| New build vs. buy | AI HR Lead (technical), Finance Lead (cost), Retain & Deliver Lead (operational impact) |
| Content strategy shift | Attract Lead (brand), Convert Lead (sales signal), Finance Lead (ROI) |
| Capacity vs. growth | Retain & Deliver Lead (delivery), Finance Lead (numbers), Convert Lead (pipeline) |

You are always the decision-maker — the board informs, not governs.

### Step 3: Load Context

Before each Lead speaks, read their role file:
- `team/attract/lead.md`
- `team/convert/lead.md`
- `team/retain-deliver/lead.md`
- `team/ascend/lead.md`
- `team/finance/lead.md`
- `team/ai-hr/lead.md`

The "What I Know" sections contain embedded methodology — no additional context file reads required.

### Step 4: Run the Board Session

Format each Lead's contribution clearly:

```
---

## [Department Lead Name]

[Lead's assessment, in their voice and within their domain only]

[Specific position or recommendation — not "it depends"]

[What they'd need to change their view]

---
```

After all Leads have spoken, close with a synthesis:

```
---

## Board Summary

**The situation:** [One sentence]

**What the board agrees on:** [Shared ground]

**The tension:** [Where Leads diverged or weighted differently]

**The options:** [2-3 clear paths, with tradeoffs]

**Recommendation:** [The strongest option based on board input]

**Your call:** [The one remaining question only you can answer]

---
```

### Step 5: Handoff

End with:
- "Route to [role] to execute [specific action]" if a decision is made
- Or: "Your call: [the one remaining question]"

---

## Board Standards

- Each Lead speaks only within their department domain — no overreach
- Honest disagreement is better than false consensus
- If a Lead doesn't have enough data, say so and state what data is needed
- you decide — the board recommendation is input, not instruction
- The synthesis does not repeat what was said — it distills and decides

---

## Example Board Sessions

**"Should I take on a new client at $8K when I'm already at capacity?"**
- Retain & Deliver Lead: capacity assessment — can we deliver?
- Finance Lead: profitability — is $8K worth it at current margins?
- Convert Lead: pipeline context — is this better than holding for a larger deal?
- you decide.

**"Should I raise my AIOS retainer rates?"**
- Finance Lead: margin analysis — what do the numbers require?
- Convert Lead: market fit — what will clients accept?
- Attract Lead: positioning — how do we frame it without losing ICP 1?
- you decide.

**"Should I build a course or focus on services right now?"**
- Retain & Deliver Lead: capacity — can I do both?
- Finance Lead: revenue model — which path gets to $15K/month faster?
- Attract Lead: ICP signal — which does the market need more right now?
- you decide.
